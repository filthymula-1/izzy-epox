Our Process

Preparation Makes the Difference.

Quality epoxy flooring requires more than simply applying a coating. The concrete must be properly evaluated, prepared and finished.

01

Evaluate

We inspect the concrete and discuss the desired style, use and condition of the space.

02

Prepare

03

Apply

Epoxy materials, colors and decorative effects are applied to create the design.

04

Finish

The completed floor is inspected for a clean, smooth and professional appearance.
